Want to see some material published?

  • Send us an email to vodary.suggerer@fias.fr describing the material, its language and graphic system and its authorship.
  • We (the open-minded editorial committee) will examine the possibility to publish it.
  • Non-trivial languages and graphic systems, unusual genres and forms are welcome.
  • If you are an author, please consider our open-access editorial guidelines.
